## akSaya-tRtIyA

अक्षय-तृतीया is observed on Śukla-Tṛtīyā tithi of Vaiśākha (lunar) month.

Daanam of Dadhyodanam, Vyanjanam, Chatram, Paduka, Udaka Kumbham, Mangos, Jackfruit; Lakshmi Narayana Pooja; Gouri Pooja;
