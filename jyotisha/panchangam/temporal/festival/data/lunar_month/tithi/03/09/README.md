## Brahmāṇī-Pūjā
ब्रह्माणी-पूजा is observed on Śukla-Navamī tithi of Jyaiṣṭha (lunar) month (sunrise (default)/puurvaviddha (default)).

_ब्रह्माणी is one of the seven मातृकाs_

## Śukla-Devī-Pūjā
