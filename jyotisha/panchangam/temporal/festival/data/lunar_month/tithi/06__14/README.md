## ananta-caturdazI

अनन्त-चतुर्दशी is observed on Śukla-Caturdaśī tithi of Bhādrapada (lunar) month (sunrise (default)/puurvaviddha (default)).

Visarjan of Ganesha idols

## ananta-padmanAbha-vratam
