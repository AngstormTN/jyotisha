## upAGga-lalitA-vratam

उपाङ्ग-ललिता-व्रतम् is observed on Śukla-Pañcamī tithi of Āśvayuja (lunar) month (sunrise (default)/puurvaviddha (default)).

Do `pUjA` of Lalita Devi.

## lalitA-paJcamI
