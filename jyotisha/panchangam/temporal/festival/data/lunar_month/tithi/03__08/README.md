## dhUmAvatI jayantI

धूमावती जयन्ती is observed on Śukla-Aṣṭamī tithi of Jyaiṣṭha (lunar) month (sunrise (default)/puurvaviddha (default)).

Goddess Dhumavati is 7th of the Dasha Maha Vidyas.
